Video Mixer Plugins¶

Video Passthrough¶

A simple video mixer plugin that takes 1 input and passes it on to the output plugin. This plugin is capable of configuring the video framerate as well.

@author: Thanh Ha

Picture-In-Picture¶

A video mixer plugin which takes 2 video sources and creates a Picture-In-Picture mode.

@author: Thanh Ha
